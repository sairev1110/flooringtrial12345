Why Armstrong Flooring?


Armstrong Flooring is an internationally recognized brand in the flooring industry, offering complete solutions from floorings, fittings, and installation training accompanied by products covering homogenous floorings.HET, LVT, VCT, SPC, wall materials, wood flooring. rubber flooring, linoleum flooring and carpets.


Armstrong Flooring, which has been in business for over 150 years and is headquartered in Lancaster, Pennsylvania, USA, is a global leader in the flooring industry. Armstrong always adheres to the philosophy of providing Eco-friendly, innovative, and high-quality flooring product designs for office, medical, and educational buildings, and provides customers with fully integrated solutions and services to meet various embroidered and functional needs of interiors in multiple areas.


We are India's most trusted wooden flooring company, operating since 2000. Flooring Impex has a vision of creating a perfect integrated flooring solution for our customers.
