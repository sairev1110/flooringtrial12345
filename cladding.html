Cladding

Cladding is the protection of the exterior of a structure.

Just like raincoats hold you dry by smoothly letting water move through their cover.

In the same manner, Cladding protects buildings, houses, and apartments from adverse climatic conditions. It regulates how elements strike and fall on a structure's surface. It shields the building's interiors from poor weather while also helping the outside elegant and appealing. The cladding system offers a useful, cost-effective solution for preserving a good and safe environment. Cladding is made of various materials including wood, metal, brick, vinyl, and composite materials. It may also comprise aluminium, wood, and composite materials.

Benefits of Cladding

Incorporating cladding to the exterior of your home or building is an intelligent choice that generates a distinct and refined structure. It looks pleasant and increases the monetary value of a building for buyers who come to look at it. It is available in a wide variety of colours and textures to suit every taste.

• Being durable and long-lasting, cladding offers a low level of maintenance as it generally requires only a scheduled wash to remain clean and in good condition.
• It aids in protection from adverse weather conditions like heat, wind, cold, rain and pollution.
• Installation of cladding on the exterior of the surface helps in thermal insulation and maintaining adequate temperature during summers.
• Cladding elevates the overall look and aesthetic of your home and makes it more appealing.
• There are a variety of options available for cladding which includes laminate, plastic, metal, vinyl, wood and many more.
• Cladding is more natural and hence reduces the chance of re-painting your building.


Types Of Cladding
Shera Cladding

Shera cladding is by far the most popular natural wood-building material in India for cladding. It is made of fibre-composite materials and is ideal for both indoor and outdoor applications. Cladding from the Shera brand has the maximum weatherproof, termite and insect-proof, and fire-resistant qualities. We offer cladding which is both cost-effective and low-maintenance. We give you the freedom to create whatever you want with the Shera brand cladding without having to sacrifice creativity or quality.

HPL Cladding

Greenlam HPL (High-Pressure Laminate) cladding features a multitude of wall cladding options. It is meant to boost the aesthetic of your exterior. Flooring Impex incorporates a wide range of green lam HPL cladding materials, such as:
• Rear Ventilated facades
• Facade Claddings
• Balcony Claddings
• Partitions
• Railings
• Fences
• Attic Cladding

Some of the prime functions of HPL cladding include easy installation, aid in insulation, and protection against water condensation, noise, pollution and heavy rain. HPL is suitable for every weather condition.

Everest Cladding

Everest cladding is renowned for being durable it helps protect the structure's outer surface from the impact of weathering elements such as heat from the sun, heavy rain, and UV rays. It ensures a high degree of resistance to fire and noise pollution. Even though it is thinner, Everest cladding has a relatively high modulus of rupture. It is stronger compared to other cladding product materials available in the market. Its design enables it to sustain maximum weight while occupying minimal space.
